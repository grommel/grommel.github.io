Teaching Philosophy

I teach physics with a focus on clarity, conceptual foundations, and mathematical rigor. I integrate computational tools such as Mathematica, Maple, Python, and Excel into my courses to help students visualize and solve complex problems. I have taught Classical Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Quantum Mechanics, Statistical Physics, and more at undergraduate and graduate levels.
