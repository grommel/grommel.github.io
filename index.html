About Me

I am a physicist with a Ph.D. in Fundamental Physics from Universidad de Los Andes, Venezuela. I have over 18 years of academic experience teaching in universities across Latin America. My areas of expertise include Classical Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Quantum Mechanics, and General Relativity. I have taught undergraduate and graduate courses, supervised numerous theses, published in peer-reviewed journals, and led multiple research projects in theoretical physics—especially in the field of brane world models and gravitational localization. I am passionate about creating rigorous yet accessible educational materials and fostering a love for physics in my students.
