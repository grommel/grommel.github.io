About Me

I am a physicist with a Ph.D. in Fundamental Physics and more than 18 years of experience in academic teaching and research. My work focuses on gravitational effects in brane world scenarios and higher-dimensional theories. I have taught physics at all university levels, authored peer-reviewed papers, supervised numerous theses, and contributed to the design and coordination of physics curricula in Latin America. I believe in rigorous, student-centered education that inspires curiosity and critical thinking.
